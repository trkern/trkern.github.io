\textbf{Claim:} Let $A,B,C$ be $n \times n$ Matrices. Then $A+B=B+A$.\\

\textbf{Proof:} In order to show that these two matrix expressions are equal, we need to show that $(A+B)_{ij} = (B+A)_{ij}$ for any indices $1 \leq i,j \leq n$.
\renewcommand{\arraystretch}{2}
\[\begin{array}{rl@{\qquad}l}
&(A+B)_{ij}\\
=&A_{ij}+B_{ij} & \text{Definition of Matrix Addition}\\
=&B_{ij}+A_{ij} & \text{Commutativity of Addition for Real Numbers}\\
=&(B+A)_{ij} & \text{Definition of Matrix Addition (Reverse)}
\end{array}\]

Thus showing that $(A+B)_{ij}=(B+A)_{ij}$ as desired.
